OUR STORY.

Welcome to UNCC Boxing Club

We are one of USA's best authentic university based boxing club.
Our journey incorporates skill progression techniques and
interval training for a true full-body workout in a 'no-judgments' environment.

Each authentic heavy bag boxing workout combines the benefits of high impact
training and stress relief, while incorporating skills and fun into each class.

UNCC Boxing Club is also an opportunity to join like-minded individuals and share in
physical and mental progress in a supportive and engaging environment.

Our People

Our people have heart. The club president, safety officers, group and fitness
experts is here to connect with you. We know your name and your story.
Better yet, we know how to produce the results you long for.

It's time to reawaken your fighting spirit.

Our Mission

Our mission at UNCC Boxing Club is to increase fitness and decrease stress
for all students at UNCC through the most authentic boxing training.
